Howard Hughes

The real Iron Man!

Howard Robard Hughes Jr., was born in Houston in the 24 of December 1905, son of Howard Robard Hughes Sr., a successfol inventor and businessman from Missouri and Allene Stone Gano member of one of the most proeminent families in Texas. At a young age Howard shows to be very interested and had great aptitude for science, technology and engineering, at age 11 he built the first wireless radio transmitter in Houston and at 12 a motorized bycicle, with parts from his father steam engine. At age 14 he came in touch with the biggest passion of his life, aviation, when he convinced his father to buy him a flight lesson, it will be a connection for all his life, ending only with his death in 1976. In 1922 the first stroke in his life, his mother dies suddenly, and then 2 years later his father also from an heart attack, who puts the young Howard(19 at the time) the major heir of Hughes Tool Company, probably the biggest manufacturer of drill bits for oil prospection in the world at the time. This events turn the young Howard in a very healthy man, and was the beginning of a fantastic life trip with business interests in entertainement, real estate, aviation and aeroespace, who turn Howard in to a billionaire and one of most proeminent and eccentric figures of the 20st century.

Personal Life
